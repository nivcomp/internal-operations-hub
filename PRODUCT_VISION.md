# Product Vision

Client-to-Scope AI is an internal operating system for Yaniv's agency work. It turns loose client conversations into structured projects with clear scope, pricing, approvals, paid hours, supplier assignments, time tracking, change requests, payments, and organized communication.

The product should reduce the operational friction between a client asking for help and a project becoming approved, profitable, paid, assigned, and deliverable.

## Primary user

The first and primary user is Yaniv.

This is not initially a public self-serve SaaS product. The first version must solve one real operator's workflow for managing clients, suppliers, costs, scope decisions, approvals, and delivery risk.

## Core jobs

- Capture new client needs in one place.
- Turn discovery notes into structured briefs and scopes.
- Keep client-facing commitments separate from supplier-facing instructions.
- Set client price and supplier cost separately.
- Track approvals, payments, and paid-hour balances before work begins.
- Assign suppliers only when the project is ready.
- Track supplier work, time, and amount owed.
- Manage change requests without allowing unapproved work to begin.
- Keep files, links, notes, and decisions attached to the correct project.
- Use AI to assist with structure, summaries, drafts, and recommendations without giving AI final business authority.

## Product principles

- The agency stays in control.
- No work starts without the required approval and payment conditions.
- Client price, supplier cost, and margin remain separate.
- A change request is not approved work until Yaniv reviews and prices it.
- The interface should be calm, simple, fast to scan, and focused on decisions and blockers.
- Build the internal operating workflow before adding advanced AI or public SaaS capabilities.

## Long-term direction

If the internal version works reliably, it may later become a repeatable agency operating system. That future possibility must not expand or distract the first MVP.
